1. Yoga Capital Of world
Known as the Tapobhumi, a place for meditation and yoga for old age and gods. This place has been a pioneer destination for people to visit and learn yoga. A perfect place for the spiritual awakening in the lap of nature, the yoga heritage of Rishikesh is pristine.

2. Lord Of The Senses
Ever wondered what the history of Rishikesh actually means. Derived from two words Hrishika means’ senses’ and isha means ‘lord’; the true meaning of the city is ‘Lord of the senses’ or is referred to Lord Vishnu.

3. Gateway To Char Dham
Seek eternal spirituality and peace by exploring the Char Dham, which are Kedarnath, Badrinath, Gangotri, and Yamunotri. A place believed to offer immemorial meditation that liberates one from a cycle of life and death. Another fact about Rishikesh is, this is the door to Char Dham Yatra.

4. Purest Place
Rishikesh is a religious destination and holy land. One of the purest places to visit, it completely bans the consumption of alcohol and non-vegetarian dishes. Even the cafes and restaurants do not serve them.

5. Highly Literate
A holy city with ashrams and yoga centres is also one of the highest literacy rates. The literacy rate in Rishikesh is around 86%. A destination for tourism is even popular to learn yoga and earn a certification worldwide.

6. Ayurvedic Treatments
The long lost tradition of ayurvedic treatment is still alive in the city. The amazing ayurvedic spas and massages not only relaxes your body but also allow your mind and body to find tranquillity.

7. Divine Ganga Aarti
The recitation of the long hymns by Brahmin Pundits on Triveni Ghat to offer prayers and flowers to Goddess Ganga creates a divine atmosphere. The shining lamps on the Ghat create a peaceful and emotional sight for every visitor.

8. 133 Year Old Kailash Ashram Brahmavidya Peetham
The ashram dedicated to promoting and preserving the traditional and classical Vedanta studies of the past is amazing. This place offered enlightenment to prominent and inspiring leaders like Swami Rana Tirtha, Swami Vivekananda, and Swami Sivananda.

9. Divine Temples And Monuments
A town is loaded with beautiful temples and monuments that are breathtaking. From the Neelkanth Mahadev Temple to Beatles Ashram and Rama Jhula to Lakshman Jhula, every sight to see is a divine and perfect beauty.

10. Dive In Adventure
A holy city is one that is beautifully decorated by nature. With innumerable adventure sports like rafting, camping, trekking, paragliding, and bungee jumping, Rishikesh is the perfect destination for adventure enthusiasts.
